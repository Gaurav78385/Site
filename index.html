ABOUT

Self-help society is a term that refers to a social movement that promotes the idea of individuals improving their own lives through self-reliance, mutual aid, and collective action. Self-help society is based on the belief that people can overcome their personal and social problems by learning from each other, sharing their experiences, and supporting one another. Self-help society is often associated with various forms of self-organization, such as self-help groups, cooperatives, community development projects, and social enterprises.
